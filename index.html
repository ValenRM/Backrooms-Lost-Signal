Backrooms: Lost Signal

About the Game

Backrooms: Lost Signal is a cooperative horror experience where survival is never guaranteed. Explore endless environments, solve environmental puzzles, and survive together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the game single-player or multiplayer?

The game supports solo play and cooperative gameplay for up to 4 players.

Does the game use voice chat?

Yes. Proximity voice chat is a core mechanic of the experience.

Which platforms are supported?

The game is currently available on PC (Windows).
