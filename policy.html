This information is used solely to improve game stability, fix bugs, and enhance the player experience.

Platform Services

Backrooms: Lost Signal is distributed through third-party platforms such as Steam and Epic Games Store. These platforms may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information:

The game includes proximity voice chat features when playing in multiplayer. Voice data is transmitted between players during gameplay but is not recorded or stored by NoSignal Games.

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data associated with the game. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Backrooms: Lost Signal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
